How To Store The Energy?

To store the energy use solid state batteries. The thing with solid state batteries is that they never lose their electrolyte strips, they use solid electrodes and solid electrolytes the solid state works with all 3 types of renewable energy sources (wind, hydro, solar)

Hydro

Solar

solar energy produces 3900 watts per 12 hours, the wind produces 10000 watts and hydroelectric produces 9600 watts per day. These energy sources provide electricity for living standards and farms.
